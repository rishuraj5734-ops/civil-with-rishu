Site Practical - Civil Engineering

1. Concrete Technology

  • Check slump before pouring concrete
  • Maintain water cement ratio
  • Ensure proper vibration and compaction
  • Take cube samples for strength test

2. Surveying

  • Check level instrument calibration
  • Take benchmark reference
  • Measure horizontal and vertical angles properly
  • Maintain field book records

3. Soil Mechanics

  • Conduct soil bearing capacity test
  • Check soil compaction
  • Perform field density test
  • Check moisture content of soil

4. RCC Construction

  • Check reinforcement spacing
  • Verify bar bending schedule
  • Ensure proper cover blocks
  • Check shuttering alignment

5. Building Construction

  • Check brick alignment
  • Maintain proper mortar ratio
  • Ensure plumb and level
  • Check curing of masonry

6. Quantity Surveying

  • Measure work quantity on site
  • Verify material consumption
  • Prepare measurement book
  • Check BOQ items
